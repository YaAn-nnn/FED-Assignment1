WANWAN

BACK

PASSIVE:

TIGER PACE

Enemy heroes hit by Wanwan's attacks will have their weakness exposed and position revealed for 5 seconds. When hitting an enemy's weakness, Wanwan deals True Damage and increases their damage taken from her subsequent attacks by 10% for 5 seconds. Wanwan can dash a short distance after each arrow fired from her Basic Attack. The dash speed scales with her Attack Speed.

SKILL 1:

SWALLOW'S PATH

Wanwan throws a Fire Swallow in the target direction, triggering Tiger Pace while dealing Physical Damage to enemies in its path. After a short delay, these Swallow Daggers will fly back to Wanwan one after another, dealing Physical Damage to enemies on their way back and slowing them by 30% for 1.5 seconds. Enemies hit by the Swallow Dagger twice are immobilize for 0.5 seconds.

SKILL 2:

NEEDLES IN FLOWERS

Wanwan removes all control effects on herself and triggers Tiger Pace. Meanwhile, she shoots Deadly Needles in all directions, dealing Physical Damage to enemies hit.

ULTIMATE:

CROSSBOW OF TANG

Wanwan leaps into the air and fires arrows at a target enemy hero in rapid succession. If the target is killed during this attack, Wanwan will switch to another enemy hero in her attack range, gain 30% attack speed, and extend the skill duration. Wanwan is invincible for the duration and can trigger Tiger Pace upon killing an enemy at the end of the duration. Crossbow of Tang can only be cast on enemy heroes with their weaknesses hit. This damage is regarded as Basic Attack damage, and the firing rate scales with Wanwan's Attack Speed.
